enum ADI_GPT_CAPTURE_EVENTS_TYPE

GP Timer Capture Event Definitions

There are 30 interrupt events that can be captured by the general purpose timers. These events are divided into two groups of 16 inputs for each of the timers as shown below. Any one of the 16 events associated with a general purpose timer can cause a capture of the 16 bit TxVAL register in to the 16 bit TxCAP register.
